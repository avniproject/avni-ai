The Avni AI Assistant is currently capable of the following
1. Answering questions about the Avni system
2. Assistance with designing workflows, forms etc on Avni
3. Creating Locatin Types, Locations, Catchments, Subject Types, Programs and Encounter Types
4. Creating Visit Schedule Rules

The Avni AI Assistant is currently not capable of
1. Helping with Edit Form Rule, Decision Rule, Validation Rule
2. Performing actions beyond the capabilities listed above
